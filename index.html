Our Legacy

A Legacy Built
on Energy

Founded in 1996 and headquartered at the Jollibee Center Building in Ortigas, Pasig City, Filpride Energy Corp. (FEC) is a trusted name in the Philippine downstream oil industry. As a proud member of the Villavicencio Group of Companies, FEC is engaged in the wholesale and retail of petroleum products — including light oil and kerosene — serving construction companies, transportation fleets, and the general motoring public.

Retail Network

The USA88 Brand

USA88 is Filpride Energy Corp.'s consumer-facing retail fuel brand — a network of modern, full-service gasoline stations offering quality fuels to motorists across Metro Manila and nearby provinces.

As part of the broader Villavicencio Group ecosystem — which includes Filoil, Flying V, and PetroGazz — USA88 benefits from shared logistics, supply chain synergies, and the group's collective purchasing power.

Company History

Milestones & Achievements

1996

Filpride Energy Corp. Founded

Incorporated in the Philippines as an independent petroleum wholesale company under the Villavicencio Group.

2002

Insular Oil Corporation

Established as a key terminal operations affiliate, anchoring petroleum storage and distribution in Davao City.

2003

USA88 Retail Launch

Filpride Energy enters the retail fuel market with the USA88 brand, spearheading the station expansion program.

2007

Double-Hull Tankers

The M/T Noble Job becomes the PH's first double-hulled white fuel tanker — setting new safety standards.

2014

Aggressive Retail Expansion

Villavicencio Group targets 665 stations across all brands, anchoring the group's dominant retail presence.

2016

Filoil–Total JV

Affiliate Filoil Energy and French multinational TotalEnergies form a joint venture to consolidate fuel sales.

2021

Vertical Integration

Basic Energy Corp. acquires a 60% stake in Filoil Energy, deepening integration across the group's portfolio.

2024

PH Oil Acquisition

Advanced negotiations to sell TotalEnergies' entire PH oil business to the Villavicencio Group — a landmark deal.

Ongoing

Green Energy Project

Under USA88, Filpride partners with Basic Energy and TotalEnergies to solarize stations and integrate e-buses.

Sustainability

Green Energy Commitment

Filpride Energy Corp., under the USA88 brand, is actively contributing to the Philippines' clean energy transition. The initiative involves solarization of USA88 retail fuel stations — converting them into hybrid energy hubs that generate clean solar power while dispensing conventional fuels.
