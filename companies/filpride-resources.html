Filpride Resources, Inc. is the core wholesale petroleum arm of the Villavicencio Group. Incorporated in the Philippines and headquartered at the Jollibee Center Building in Ortigas, Pasig City, the company serves as the anchor for the Group's downstream oil operations.

The company is engaged in the purchase and resale of petroleum products — including automotive gasoline, diesel, kerosene, and aviation fuels — supplying construction companies, industrial firms, transportation fleets, and independent fuel retailers across the archipelago.

As a proud member of the Independent Philippine Petroleum Companies Association (IPPCA), Filpride Resources upholds industry standards in safety, compliance, and supply chain reliability.

Operations

Supply Chain
& Distribution

Filpride Resources operates through a network of strategic terminal affiliates — including Insular Oil Corporation in Davao City — to ensure nationwide product availability and timely delivery.

The company's proprietary tanker fleet, which includes the Philippines' first double-hulled white fuel tanker (M/T Noble Job, commissioned in 2007), enables safe and efficient interisland petroleum transport.

Filpride Resources' logistics capabilities support not only its own group brands (USA88, Flying V, PetroGazz) but also third-party retail partners that rely on consistent, quality fuel supply.
