Overview

Real Estate Development

Bienes de Oro is the real estate development arm of the Villavicencio Group. The company focuses on mid- to high-end residential developments, mixed-use projects, and commercial property management across Metro Manila and key provincial cities.

Leveraging the Group's financial strength and existing land bank, Bienes de Oro develops properties that offer long-term value to homebuyers, investors, and commercial tenants alike.

Operations

Development Pipeline

Current projects include residential condominium developments in Pasig and Quezon City, as well as a mixed-use township project in Rizal province that integrates retail, office, and residential components.

Bienes de Oro's property management division handles leasing, facility maintenance, and tenant relations for its completed developments, ensuring consistent returns and tenant satisfaction.
