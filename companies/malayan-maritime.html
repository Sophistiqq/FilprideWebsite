Overview

Maritime Fuel Transport

Malayan Maritime Services, Inc. is the maritime logistics subsidiary of the Filpride Group of Companies, specializing in the coastal and interisland transport of petroleum products, lubricants, and bulk liquids across the Philippine archipelago.

The company operates a fleet of modern tanker vessels — including the Philippines' first double-hulled white fuel tanker, M/T Noble Job — ensuring the highest standards of environmental protection and cargo safety.

Operations

Fleet & Routes

Malayan Maritime's vessels operate on regular routes connecting major petroleum terminal hubs in Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao, supporting both the Group's own fuel brands and third-party oil companies.

The company maintains MARINA-certified crews and vessels, and operates in full compliance with MARPOL and SOLAS international maritime conventions for environmental and safety performance.
