Overview

Inland Waterway Logistics

The Barge company is a specialized inland and coastal waterway transport company under the Filpride Group of Companies. Its fleet of purpose-built barges serves petroleum distribution along river systems and shallow coastal routes across the Philippines.

Barge transport offers a cost-effective and environmentally efficient alternative to road hauling for bulk fuel delivery to remote industrial sites, mining operations, and island communities.

Operations

Operational Scope

The company's vessels are designed for flexibility — capable of carrying petroleum products, lubricants, and general bulk cargo depending on client requirements and seasonal waterway conditions.

Safety protocols include double-containment systems, continuous GPS tracking, and certified crew competency standards aligned with MARINA requirements for inland waterway operations.
