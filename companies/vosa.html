Overview

Group Support Services

Vosa is a specialized services company within the Villavicencio Group. It provides critical operational support functions — including procurement, IT infrastructure, and shared services — that underpin the Group's diverse portfolio of businesses.

By centralizing non-core functions under a single entity, Vosa enables the Group's operating companies to focus on their core competencies while benefiting from economies of scale in administrative and support operations.

Operations

Service Lines

Vosa's service portfolio spans corporate procurement, fleet management support, information technology systems management, and human resources shared services for Group-affiliated companies.

The company continuously invests in digital transformation initiatives, including ERP system integration and data analytics platforms that give Group leadership real-time visibility into operational and financial performance.
