Overview

Holding & Investments

Syvill serves as a strategic holding entity within the Villavicencio Group, overseeing a portfolio of real estate assets, investment positions, and corporate governance activities that support the Group's long-term growth objectives.

Operating as the Group's internal asset management arm, Syvill facilitates inter-company capital allocation, ensuring that the Group's diverse businesses are adequately financed and strategically aligned.

Operations

Portfolio Management

Syvill's real estate portfolio includes commercial office spaces and land holdings primarily in Metro Manila and key regional centers, providing stable rental income streams that offset the cyclical nature of the petroleum business.

The entity also holds strategic stakes in affiliated companies across the Group, acting as a conduit for dividends, treasury management, and mergers and acquisitions activity.
