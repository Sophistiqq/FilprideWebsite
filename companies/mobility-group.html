Overview

Fleet & Mobility Solutions

Mobility Group Corp. is the Villavicencio Group's dedicated fleet management and transportation company. Serving both private and government clients, the company delivers end-to-end mobility solutions across Metro Manila and provincial corridors.

From fuel supply chain integration to fleet leasing and driver services, Mobility Group Corp. provides a comprehensive suite of transport logistics solutions that complement the Group's energy portfolio.

Operations

Fleet Operations

The company manages a diverse fleet spanning light commercial vehicles, heavy trucks, and specialized petroleum transport units. Its operational synergy with Filpride Resources ensures competitive fuel pricing and streamlined maintenance schedules.

Mobility Group Corp.'s driver training and compliance programs uphold the highest road safety standards, aligning with LTFRB and DOTr guidelines across all operational routes.
