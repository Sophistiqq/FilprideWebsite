Overview

Port & Container Operations

MCY Container Terminal and Logistics is the port operations company of the Villavicencio Group, providing container handling, warehousing, and inter-island logistics services at key Philippine ports.

The company's integrated logistics model bridges the gap between maritime cargo movement and last-mile land distribution, making it an essential link in the Group's nationwide petroleum and commodity supply chain.

Operations

Terminal & Warehousing

MCY operates modern container handling equipment and a digitized yard management system that ensures accurate, real-time cargo tracking from vessel arrival to gate-out.

The terminal's bonded warehousing facilities serve both import and export clients, with dedicated cold-chain and hazardous goods handling capabilities compliant with MARINA and BOC regulations.
